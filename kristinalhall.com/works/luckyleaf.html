Lucky Leaf®

Responsive Website

My Involvement: Front-End Development, JavaScript/jQuery
Project Manager: John Caldwell
Designer: Marie Bowers & Chris Jesowicz
Responsive: John Kramer
Back-End: Michael Bonetti

This is another responsive website that was built from scratch for Lucky Leaf. I handled the front-end development as well as customizing and adjusting the JavaScript to make it all function properly with responsive design.

This site was a joint effort between many different front-end developers and was able to meet it's very short deadline. The recipes are setup in a custom database that is editable via an admin panel and was handled by Michael Bonetti. The responsive media queries were handled primary by John Kramer.

This site was very challenging due to the short deadline and the amount of content and different styled pages that were included. The slideshow on the homepage, as well as the bottom recipe portion are "swipeable" on touch devices and respond smoothly and quickly.
