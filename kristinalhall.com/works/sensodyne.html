Sensodyne

Global websites/Localization

My Involvement: Front-End Development, Back-End Development, Maintenance
Project Manager: Priscilla Beal-Leuch
Front-End/Back-End: Kyle Zielinsky
Back-End: Sabrina Shaner, Sam Benedict
Maintenance: Kyle Zielinksy, Nick Meloy, Chris Jezowicz, Matt Trout, Steven Boothe, Michael Legacy

This is an on-going project that started in 2011 with the redesign and rebuild of the Sensodyne sites. There is a base shell that is used for all new builds and new additions are being added on a regular basis and rolled out to all of the different markets. There are currently 42 Sensodyne sites for different countries and regions with several more in production. Many of these 42 sites have us maintain them and build new pieces for new product launches.

One of the newest additions to this site is the Ask a Dentist video FAQ tool as well as a brand-new responsive upgrade. This is a very large account and has a very large team upgrading and maintaining it. There are currently 7 different variations to choose from for the HTML5 homepage animation that feature several different products. It was built to use a asp.Net-based Content Management System for easy maintainence but pieces are added to the system on a regular basis.
