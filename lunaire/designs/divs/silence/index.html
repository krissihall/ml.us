about the layout
Yet another really simple design. This time I just wanted something really clean and easy on the eye. I think this is both, with no colors being too dominant and overall a soft pastel type feel. This is ideal for a domain or personal site. I rather love this design, I think I may open a site just to use it :p. I also love the coloring used on her

This layout uses  tables as it's base with div support and rather heavy css usage. It features the singer Amerie. I used my own brushes in designing this and one of my patterns. The background pattern came from 77 words on livejournal.

 

usage
The only file you need to edit in this zip is index.html. In index.html, only the parts marked "Content" and "Navbar" should be edited, and nothing else. Please be aware that it relies heavily on cascading style sheets (css) so you follow the basic setup of this page and use it as the template for your other pages. Also don't forget to upload the stylesheet or your layout won't work properly.
